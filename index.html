Our Story

Founded in 1998 by the Sharma family, Tandoori Inn brings the authentic flavors of Northern India to your table. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the traditional cooking techniques that make Indian cuisine so distinctive.

Every dish at Tandoori Inn tells a story of culture, heritage, and passion. Our chefs, trained in the culinary traditions of India, use only the freshest ingredients and hand-ground spices to create an unforgettable dining experience.
